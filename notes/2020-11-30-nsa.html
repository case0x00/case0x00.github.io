global intelligence surveillance -- NSA

goal: map out the structure of operations of the united states national security agency for fun since it seems interesting. obviously i am missing a large number of operations, programs, software, and databases. the information accuracy is also questionable and likely not up-to-date

Signals Intelligence Systems and Programs

  • SHAMROCK -- 1945-1975

  • MINARET -- 1960-1973
    • project started in 1969, the watch list existed since 1960

  • Drug Watch Lists -- 1970-1973

  • FROSTING
    • ECHELON -- 1971-present
      • intercepting intelsat satellite transmissions
      • SILKWORTH, SIRE, VORTEX (satellites)
    • TRANSIENT
      • intercepting soviet satellite transmission

  • RAMPART-A -- 1992-present
    • gaining access to high capacity international fiberoptic cables at major congestion point
    • over 3Tb/s in 2013 of voice, fax, telex, modem, email internet chat, VPN, VoIP, and voice call records through XKEYSCORE

  • THINTHREAD -- 1996-2001

  • STELLAR WIND -- 2001-2004

  • TRAILBLAZER -- 2001-2006

  • Total Information Awareness/Terrorism Information Awareness -- 2003-2003
    • predictive policing, the biggest surveillance program in the United States
    • Genoa -- DARPA project for analyzing large amounts of data and metadata for CT
    • Genoa II/Topsail
    • Genisys -- virtual data aggregation across hetereogeneous databases
    • Scalable Social Network Analysis (SSNA) -- modeling key characteristics of terrorist groups
    • Evidence Extraction and Link Discovery (EELD) -- automated discovery, extraction, and linking of sparse evidence

  • TURBULENCE -- 2005-present

  • Upstream Collection -- intercepting telephone and internet traffic from the internet backbone
    • FAIRVIEW -- coperation with AT&T in room 641A with international cables, routers, switches
      • 8 internet peering points
      • 26 VoIP router facilities
      • 1 VoIP hub router facility
      • 9 submarine cable landing points
      • 16 4ESS circuit switching stations
    • BLARNEY -- top-level communication facilities
      • US-984 -- FISA collection
      • US-984X -- FAA collection
    • STORMBREW -- cooperation with Verizon
      • US-3140 MADCAPOCELOT -- DNI (digital netowrk intelligence) and metadata through XKEYSCORE, PINWALE, and MARINA
      • US-983 STORMBREW -- DNR (dial number recognition) and DNI with Verizon
    • OAKSTAR
      • US-3206 MONKEYROCKET -- Middle East, Europe, and Asia CT, collecting DNI metadata and content
      • US-3217 SHIFTINGSHADOW -- Afghanistan communication, collecting DNR metadata
      • US-3230 ORANGECRUSH -- collecting voice, fax, DNI, DNR, and metadata with access through PRIMECANE and 3rd party partner
      • US-3247 YACHTSHOP -- worldwide DNI metadata with access through BLUEANCHOR partner
      • US-3251 ORANGEBLOSSOM
      • US-3273 SILVERZEPHYR -- South, Central, and Latin America, collecting DNR metadata, voice and fax, DNI content and metadata with access through STEELKNIGHT partner
      • US-3277 BLUEZEPHYR
      • US-3354 COBALTFALCON

  • PRISM US-984XN -- 2007-present
    • collects DNI from various US internet companies, constitutes 91% of NSA internet traffic acquired
    • Microsoft (2007), Yahoo! (2008), Google (2009), Facebook (2009), Paltalk (2009), YouTube (2010), AOL (2011), Skype (2011), Apple (2012)
    • feeds into TRAFFICTHIEF, MARINA, MAINWAY, FALLOUT, PINWALE, CONVEYANCE, NUCLEON

  • SSO Corporate/TAO Shaping -- present
    • US-3105S1 DARKTHUNDER
    • US-3105S1 STEELFLAUTA

  • DROPMIRE -- present
    • diplomatic spying

  • STATEROOM -- present
    • diplomatic spying

  • BULLRUN -- present
    • decryption

  • MYSTIC -- 2009-present
    • SOMALGET -- recording and archiving of phone calls

  • TEMPORA -- present
    • collaboration with GCHQ similar to upstream collection

software

  • Unified Targeting Tool (UTT)
    • part of PRISM
    • replaced OCTAVE in 2011

  • XKEYSCORE
    • DNI exploitation system/analytic framework
    • servers are fed data from F6 (CIA and NSA), FORNSAT (foreign satellite collection), NSA SSO, Overhead (spyplanes, drones, satellites), NSA TAO, FISA, and third party countries

  • POISONNUT
    • Virtual Private Network Attack Orchestrator (VAO)
    • clients:
      • TURMOIL
      • WEALTHYCLUSTER
    • each client can connect using ISLANDTRANSPORT, and receive/send encrypted XML messages using ISLANDHIDEAWAY

  • SHELLTRUMPET -- 2007-present 1
    • near realtime metadata analyzer for the CLASSIC collection system
