AZUREVIPER

about

AZUREVIPER will be a single-wheeled mobile robot (ballbot). Essentially it is an inverted pendulum, with the only point of contact with the ground being with a ball.
  • v1 will be 600mm tall consisting of the ball and x3 motors for moving the ball. The sole purpose will be to verify the control for stability.
  • v2 will be 1600mm tall consisting of the v1 lower and an upper with 2x 4DOF arms and a head with stereovision
