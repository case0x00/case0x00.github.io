DARKDYNAMO

about

DARKDYNAMO will be a very long legged tripedal robot. Tripedalism is interesting given it doesn't occur in nature, due to all animals that use their legs for locomotion having bilateral symmetry for aerodynamic purposes. Those animals that are radially symmetric tend to be ocean dwelling and typically float (jellyfish) or otherwise don't really use their appendages for walking-like locomotion (starfish).

My inspiration is from UCLA's RoMeLa lab (image is from lab, it is not mine).
