monsoon

i recently watched prospect (2018) and its a highly immersive world; the designs of the ships, the designs of the space suits and the reason why they are even on that world are such intriguing concepts. it got me thinking about space westerns, the frontier in space, and exploring the unknown in its purest form. i think this is what i want monsoon to be. that idea is really the reason why i got into aerospace engineering in the first place -- exploring the unknown.
